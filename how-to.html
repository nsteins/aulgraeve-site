How to Aul Graeve

So you want to celebrate Aul Graeve? While the true spirit of Aul Graeve is the friends we made along the way, the list that follows offers a few traditional items to consider that may make the experience more festive.

  • String lights
  • Evergreen

  • Strings of dried chili peppers
